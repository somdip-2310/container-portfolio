Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated on Nov 26 2025

AAR Labs is committed to providing quality services through SnapDeploy. This policy outlines terms for refunds and cancellations.

1. Free Tier

Our Free Tier includes 200 hours of runtime per month at no cost. As this is free, no refunds are applicable.

2. Paid Subscriptions

2.1 No Refunds on Monthly Subscriptions

All monthly subscription fees are non-refundable. When you cancel, you retain access until the current billing cycle ends.

2.2 Exceptions

Refunds may be considered for:

  • Service outages preventing usage for extended periods
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors
  • Service not delivered as promised

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1 How to Cancel

Cancel anytime through your account settings or email contact@snapdeploy.dev.

3.2 Effect of Cancellation

  • Subscription remains active until billing cycle ends
  • Access to paid features retained until paid period ends
  • Account downgrades to Free Tier after paid period
  • No refund for remaining days in billing cycle

4. Upgrades and Downgrades

4.1 Upgrades

  • Takes effect immediately
  • Prorated charge for remainder of billing cycle

4.2 Downgrades

  • Takes effect at end of current billing cycle
  • No refund for plan difference
